Brand Intro.

Dove, sold under the name "Galaxy" in the UK, is the chocolate brand name of Mars Food Company.

The brand name comes from a Chicago candy store established in 1956. It entered the US market in 1985. Acquired by Mars Food in 1986. Entered the Chinese mainland market in 1989. In 1995, it became China's leading chocolate brand, and "milk is fragrant and silky" became its classic advertising slogan. On September 14, 2023, Dove's official Weibo announced that the Moutai liquor chocolate in cooperation with Moutai will be launched on September 16. On September 16, 2023, Moutai Dove's joint product “Mao Xiaoling” wine-filled chocolate was officially launched

Campaign Intro.

"Your Pleasure Has Promise"

On March 8, 2023, Mars Wrigley's Dove brand announced its global new purpose - "Your Pleasure Has Promise" with the launch ceremony of Dove All-Women Academy held in Baiyan county, Southwest China's Guizhou Province. By 2030, Dove is committed to helping one million women and their families to achieve prosperity and development. In China, by launching the Dove All-Women Academy partnering with the leading NGO China Foundation for Rural Development, Dove China is committed to empowering Chinese women with knowledge and skills for their development.
